Our story

Family-Owned Auto Care Since 1995

To provide honest, high-quality auto repairs with transparent communication and fair pricing, earning the trust of every customer.

El Mundo’s Auto Service was founded in 1995 by Miguel Hernandez with a simple vision: create a shop where Dallas drivers could get reliable repairs without the stress. Starting with just two bays and a commitment to doing the job right the first time, the business quickly grew through word-of-mouth. Today, we operate from a full-service facility on N Haskell Ave, serving thousands of loyal customers.

Over the years, we’ve invested in the latest diagnostic equipment and continuous training for our team. But some things haven’t changed: our dedication to transparency, our respect for every customer’s time and budget, and our love for keeping Dallas moving. We treat every car as if it belongs to our own family.
